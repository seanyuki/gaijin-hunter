Description

Kansai International Academy operates nine campuses in Kansai and six in Tokyo, offering bilingual education through kindergartens and early learning centers. Our Kobe campus is authorized for the IB Primary Years, Middle Years, and Diploma Programmes, and is accredited by the Council of International Schools (CIS).

We are seeking passionate and experienced teachers to join our Early Years and Kindergarten programs (ages 1–6). This is a full-year teaching position, working closely with a bilingual Japanese teaching partner to support children’s holistic development.

Some positions may begin earlier with training provided. Mid-year hires may start as substitute teachers before taking on a full class the following school year. As we are not an English conversation school, applicants must be ready for the challenges of holistic education and committed to their own professional development.

Key Responsibilities:
⚫︎ Full-time teaching duties and classroom management
⚫︎ Assessing and tracking student progress
⚫︎ Providing care and supervision, including toilet training and diaper changes
⚫︎ Supporting curriculum implementation, review, and development

Requirements / Skills:
⚫︎ Bachelor’s degree (Education or Early Childhood preferred)
⚫︎ Teaching certification or early childhood qualification strongly preferred
⚫︎ Experience teaching or caring for young children
⚫︎ Valid visa permitting full-time employment in Japan (student/working holiday visas not accepted)

Compensation:
In addition to the monthly salary, eligible candidates receive travel expense reimbursement, health insurance, and relocation support. Teachers with a BA or BSc in Education also receive a monthly qualification allowance.

NOTE:
Please submit a cover letter addressed to Kansai International Academy, explaining your interest in our school and how your skills fit our philosophy. This is not an entry-level role; ESL experience alone is not sufficient. Applicants should show clear understanding of our programs and values. Due to the number of applications, only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

The above information is a public job ad. This job ad is neither an offer nor a contract.

About Kansai International Academy:
Established in 2001, Kansai International Academy (KIA) is a bilingual school with 14 campuses across the Kansai and Kanto regions of Japan. We offer a unique learning environment that blends international best practices with Japanese cultural values to nurture the next generation of global leaders.

Each campus includes an Early Learning Center (ages 1–3) and a Kindergarten (ages 3–6). Our Kobe campus is an IB Continuum School, offering the Primary Years Programme (PYP), Middle Years Programme (MYP), and Diploma Programme (DP), and is accredited by the Council of International Schools (CIS). Sakura International School, TokyoMinami Azabu campus is an Elementary School authorized as an IB World School for the PYP.

Our bilingual structure, multilingual perspectives, and immersion in Japanese culture create a rich and well-rounded learning experience. We maintain low student-to-teacher ratios, allowing teachers to build meaningful relationships with students and tailor learning to individual needs.

Our Mission:
Developing creative and responsible leaders who can contribute to global society through inquiry-based Japanese-English bilingual education.

Our Vision:
KIA is committed to creating a safe and nurturing learning environment that fosters the development of emotionally resilient and open-minded learners, inspiring them to collaborate and take responsibility for their own words and actions as individuals and as members of the community.

Our educators come from around the world, bringing with them diverse perspectives and a shared passion for teaching and learning. At KIA, you’ll become part of a supportive and collaborative team, contributing to a meaningful mission while growing professionally in a vibrant, multicultural environment. If you're flexible, enthusiastic, and passionate about working with children, we warmly invite you to join us.
