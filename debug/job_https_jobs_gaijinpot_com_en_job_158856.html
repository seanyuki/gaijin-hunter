Part Time Daycare Support (Nagoya)

Kinder Kids International Preschool (キンダーキッズインターナショナル)

Education / Teaching
Function
Child Education, Pre-school Teacher
Work Type
Part Time / Entry Level
Salary

Watch video presentation

Requirements

  • English: Fluent (preferred)
  • Must currently reside in Japan
  • Physically fit and able to walk, run, carry children with ease.
  • Comfortable supporting children’s personal care needs, including nappy changing and hygiene maintenance.
  • Previous experience in a school setting (preschool age and above) preferred.
  • A valid visa with work permissions
  • Be available in the afternoon between 12:30p.m. - 18:30 on weekdays (Monday-Friday, minimum 12 hours a week)

Description

Join Kinder Kids International School (KKIS): Make a Global Impact in Early Childhood Education!

Thank you for your interest in Kinder Kids International School (KKIS), a leading global educational institution with locations in Japan, Canada, and Hawaii. We are expanding our team and looking for enthusiastic individuals to join our part-time daycare staff at our Nagoya (Irinaka Station) and Noritake schools. If you're passionate about working with young children and have afternoons free, this is the perfect opportunity for you!

About the Role:
Our shifts are exclusively between 12:30 PM - 6:30 PM (no morning or weekend shifts available)

Responsibilities:

Supervise nap times, park play, arts and crafts, and snacks.
Attend to basic care needs.
Engage with children in a supportive, English-speaking environment.

Requirements:

Valid Japan visa permitting work.
Proficiency in English.
Positive, collaborative attitude and good communication skills.
Reliable transportation.
Customer service experience is a plus.
Passion for improving children's education and care.

Bonus Skills:

Musical proficiency.
Ability to speak Japanese, Mandarin Chinese, or other languages.
First aid qualifications.
Preschool, Kindergarten, or Early Childhood Education (ECE) qualifications.

What We Offer:
Hourly Wage: ¥1,200 starting, up to ¥1,230 based on performance.
Transportation Coverage: Up to ¥40,000.
Paid Leave: Based on days worked.
Social Security Enrollment: For those working 20+ hours/week (conditions apply).
Career Growth: Potential to progress to full-time roles such as Class Assistant or Lead Class Teacher.
Supportive Environment: Excellent training program and support network.
Global Experience: Work in a truly international educational setting.

Application Process:
We seek passionate individuals who bring fun and excellence to the classroom. To apply, submit your resume with a cover letter outlining why you want to work with us and what makes you unique.

Important Notes:

Applicants must reside in Japan with a valid work visa.
Specify your shift availability in your application.
Social security enrollment applies to those working 20+ hours/week (conditions apply).

Ready to Make a Difference?
If you're ready to embrace a rewarding challenge and positively impact young minds, apply today and join the Kinder Kids International School team!

Apply Now:

We look forward to receiving your application. If successful, you'll be invited to an interview, either in-person or online.

Make a Difference with KKIS!

About

We're Kinder Kids Group, a diverse team passionate about Early Childhood Education in Japan, Singapore, Canada, and the USA. Since 2000, we've operated preschools and elementary schools while developing English language content. With a global presence and a team of 65+ nationalities, we prepare students for a globalized world while respecting diverse cultures. Our unique, independent curriculum emphasizes safety, imagination, and exploration. We're committed to making positive community contributions and modeling excellence as we shape the future of society.


Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

We believe that everyone should have a fair shot at achieving their full potential, no matter where they come from. That's why we're committed to fostering a culture of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DE&I) through a variety of policies and initiatives.

We're not just paying lip service to DE&I - we're living it every day, just like the kinder kids group does! Our team is made up of people from all walks of life, and we embrace the unique perspectives, experiences, and knowledge that each person brings to the table.

As a Japanese company, we're especially proud of our diverse culture and the rich traditions that inform our approach to business. By promoting DE&I, we're not just creating a more equitable workplace - we're also building a stronger, more resilient company that can thrive in an ever-changing world.
